Become a Living Donor

Share Life Through Living Donation

ODEH carefully guides potential living donors through every step, protecting your health while helping patients in desperate need.

favorite Living Donor Overview

Becoming a living donor is a deeply personal decision. Our role is to provide clear information, connect you with top physicians, and ensure that every halachic and medical question is answered with sensitivity.

  • check_circle Comprehensive medical evaluation with highly specialized transplant teams.
  • check_circle Guidance for donors and families on risks, recovery, and long-term follow up.
  • check_circle Close coordination with Rabbinic and medical authorities throughout the process.

edit_note Start Your Application

To express interest in becoming a living donor, please complete the secure form below. An ODEH representative will contact you to review next steps.

Open Living Donor Form open_in_new

The form is hosted securely by Jotform. Please fill in as much detail as you comfortably can; all information is kept confidential.

Questions?

Call our medical guidance line at 845-666-ODEH (6334).

Halachic Guidance

We work closely with recognized Rabbinic authorities to ensure every step follows halachic standards.
