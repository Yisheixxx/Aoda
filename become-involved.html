Become Involved

Stand With Patients and Donors

There are many ways to support ODEH beyond living donation. Volunteer, fundraise, or place a pushka in your home or business and help us bring hope to more families.

volunteer_activism

Volunteer at Drives

Help staff tables, greet participants, and distribute information at donor awareness events and community programs.

volunteer_activism

Fundraising

Organize a parlor meeting, campaign, or online fundraiser to support ODEH’s core services and patient assistance.

savings

Pushka / Donation Box

Place an ODEH pushka in your home, office, or store and dedicate small daily giving to life-saving work.

Tell Us How You’d Like to Help

Choose one or more ways to get involved, and share any additional talents or resources you can offer.

  • arrow_right Volunteer at drives: logistics, setup, hospitality, translation, or outreach.
  • arrow_right Fundraising: host events, sponsor materials, or connect us with donors.
  • arrow_right Pushka: place a collection box in your home, shul, or business.

Someone from ODEH will reach out to discuss opportunities that fit your schedule and strengths.
