Host a Donor Testing Drive

Partner with ODEH to organize a donor testing drive in your school, shul, organization, or neighborhood. Help save lives by facilitating donor matching through organized testing events.

biotech Testing Drive Details

Our testing drives make it simple for community members to get tested as potential organ or bone marrow donors. ODEH will coordinate all logistics, testing materials, and follow-up to identify life-saving matches.

  • check_circle Simple cheek swab or blood testing for donor registry matching.
  • check_circle Complete coordination with testing labs and donor registries.
  • check_circle On-site staff to guide participants through the testing process.
  • check_circle Halachically sensitive protocols tailored to your community's needs.

info How It Works

Testing drives typically involve a quick, painless cheek swab that takes just minutes. Results are added to donor registries where they can help match patients in need of kidney, bone marrow, or other transplants. All testing is confidential and coordinated with appropriate medical and rabbinic oversight.
